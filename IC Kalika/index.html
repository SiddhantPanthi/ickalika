Interact Club Of Kalika

Interact Club Of Kalika is a community based Interact Club under Interact District 3292 Nepal & Bhutan sponsored by Rotary Club of Butwal , bringing together young people of age group 12-18 to develop leadership skills while discovering the power of Service Above Self. Interact Club of Kalika has done a numerous programmes which have made our club come to a limelight within such short period of time.

View more

What is Interact Kalika?

Interact Club of Kalika is a community based club sponsered by Rotary Club Of Butwal & managed by childern of age group 12-18 with the motive of service above self.

Why choose Interact Kalika?

Because IC Kalika helps children develop different skills like personality skill , leadership skill & many more and it also provides platform to showcase your skills & talents.

Something About Us

We are a group of skilled & passionated individual serving the community with the slogan 'Interact For Youth'.We are a more than a organization where we treat each other like our family members.We are here to change our community by doing radical movements.

Our Vision

Our vision is to change the community/society with the help of tiny hands & make majority of the childern skilled & capable.
